Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our web design services and process.

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline for website development depends on the complexity and scope of your project. A standard business website typically takes 4-6 weeks from initial consultation to launch. E-commerce sites and more complex projects may take 8-12 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation and keep you updated throughout the entire process. Rush projects can be accommodated with premium pricing.

What's included in your website design packages?

Our comprehensive packages include custom design, responsive mobile optimization, SEO setup, content management system, contact forms, social media integration, SSL security certificate, and 30 days of post-launch support. We also provide training on how to update your website content, basic analytics setup, and performance optimization. Additional features like e-commerce functionality, advanced SEO, and ongoing maintenance are available as add-ons.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive ongoing support and maintenance packages to keep your website secure, updated, and performing optimally. Our maintenance services include regular software updates, security monitoring, backup management, content updates, performance optimization, and technical support. We offer different tiers of support plans to match your needs and budget, with response times ranging from 24 hours to same-day for urgent issues.
